Pressa

Web App / Competition

Pressa is a simple and easy to use web app that enables autistic children express themselves. Using a collection of common words, minimalistic icons, and a text to speech API, Pressa brings out an autistic childâ€™s Inner Voice. Simply press on a combination of word tiles to form a phrase, then press the Speaker Icon for Pressa to read the phrase aloud. This app was created in less than two weeks for the 2014 Autism Appjam competition at UCI.
