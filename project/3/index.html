The new portfolio site, t/n, reflects on my latest front-end experience and user experience (UX) knowledge. With this new site, I put a lot of emphases on design workflow and code architecture.

  • Infrastructure architecture of the whole site
  • A way to innovate interaction
  • The workflow

The Workflow

To begin a project, I start off by thinking about the interaction of the whole site. What interaction would be unique and keep users engage to the site. I drew out multiple versions of different interaction to see which one is most intuitive and innovative as well as the implementation process.

After defining the interaction model, I begin to dive into the layout of the page. What content goes here ? What happen if I click or hover on this component ?
